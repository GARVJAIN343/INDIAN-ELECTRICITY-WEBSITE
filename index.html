Tips to save electricity Bill

First of all, you would need to replace all your incandescent bulbs and CFLs with LEDs. When LEDs were first introduced, they did cost a fortune. But today, due to the efforts of EESL and BEE, and a huge increase in the number of manufacturers, LEDs are available at a dirt cheap price. So, if you have not yet replaced your old bulbs and lights with LEDs, do it immediately. And because of its low price, the recovery period would be very low.The next appliance that would require your attention would be the refrigerator. Most Indians have a refrigerator at home which would be more than 10 years old. And even if you buy a new one, it is highly likely that you would still retain the old one. But refrigerators are big power guzzlers. More so, the older ones. So if you are even a tiny bit concerned about your electricity bills, sell your 10 year old refrigerator immediately.
